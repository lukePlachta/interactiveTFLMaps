Hello, thank you for viewing my maps of the rail services operating in the UK today. These pages have been coded using mark up languages of HTML and CSS. Eventually I do hope to have this page linked to pages of other national rail lines that will have been made in the same way. This site will be relaunched each time I have fully created a new map and a proper opening page and main menu pages will also be implemented in due course. On each map page, you will be able to access the page of another route by selecting that route's name at any interchange station it shares with the line you are currently viewing. Please let me know what you think and do not hesitate to contact me if you have any thoughts or suggestions.


This site has been created on a Windows 10 laptop in the resolution of 1920 by 1080 and can also be viewed on the following resolutions following the use of media queries.

  • 1680 by 1050
  • 1600 by 900
  • 1440 by 900
  • 1366 by 768
  • 1280 by 800
  • 1152 by 864
  • 1024 by 768
  • 800 by 600
